Installation
------------

Extract the archive and Upload the /RedLine/ folder into 
your 'wp-content/themes' directory.

1.  Login to your WordPress Admin and navigate to the Apperance Tab.
2.  Select RedLine theme and Activate it.
3.  That's it!
